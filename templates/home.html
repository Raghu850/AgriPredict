AgriPredict: A Decision Support System for Crop Profitability

The goal of the "AgriPredict" project is to predict crop market prices (categorized as High, Medium, or Low) before planting, using features such as historical prices, seasonal factors, weather conditions, and supply and demand indicators. By providing farmers with insights into which crops are likely to be more profitable, this system aims to help reduce farmer suicides caused by market failures.
